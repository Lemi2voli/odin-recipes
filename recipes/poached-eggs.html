Poached Eggs

classic poached eggs breakfast

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • salt
  • butter
  • toast

Steps

  1. boil water
  2. salt water
  3. stir water with spoon to create whirlpool
  4. drop eggs in with spatula
  5. boil for 10 minutes
  6. while eggs are boiling toast bread
  7. serve with butter and salt
